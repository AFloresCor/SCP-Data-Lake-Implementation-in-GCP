== Executive Summary

[.lead]
The *{project-name}* consolidates the organization's data assets into a
single, governed, and scalable platform on Google Cloud Platform, enabling faster
decision-making, cost-efficient analytics, and a foundation for more complex data projects, including machine learning.

=== The Business Problem

[sidebar]
.icon:briefcase[] Business Overview
--
Our data was scattered across dozens of isolated systems — departmental databases,
third-party SaaS exports, and manual spreadsheets. Analysts spent up to *60% of
their time* just locating and preparing data instead of generating insights.
Leadership lacked a single, reliable source of truth for key business metrics.

Aside from that, a new project was on the horizon that would require integrating data from hundreds of new sources. 
The existing data landscape was not only inefficient but also a barrier to innovation.

--

Before this project, the organization faced three critical data challenges:

. *Data Silos* — Each business unit maintained its own data store with no cross-team
  visibility or shared governance.
. *Slow Time-to-Insight* — Producing a consolidated report required days of manual
  data extraction and reconciliation.
. *Low innovation* — The fragmented data landscape made it difficult to implement data-driven projects, especially 
  those involving machine learning.

=== The Solution

We designed and implemented a centralized *Data Lake* on Google Cloud Platform using
a multi-layer architecture (Bronze → Silver → Gold) that ingests raw data from all
source systems, transforms it for quality and consistency, and serves curated datasets
to analysts, dashboards, and machine learning workflows. 

New data sources can be onboarded in days instead of months, and business users have self-service access to trusted data.

=== Strategic Value

[sidebar]
.icon:briefcase[] Business Overview
--
This platform is not just a storage system — it is a *strategic data asset*
that accelerates every team that depends on data. It is the basis on which *{company}* 
can build complex data projects, ride the wave of AI, and maintain a competitive edge in the market.
--

The data lake delivers compounding value over time:

* *Short-term* — Eliminate manual data wrangling and reduce reporting backlog.
* *Medium-term* — Enable cross-domain and multi-source analytics that were previously impossible.
* *Long-term* — Provide the data foundation for predictive analytics and AI-driven products.
